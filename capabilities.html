FAQ

Capabilities, Answered

View All Queries →
What presses does Platinum Press run?−
Two high-speed Koenig and Bauer Rapida offset presses, the same manufacturer whose equipment prints most of the world's currency, alongside flexographic and VOC/PFAS-free digital platforms, all under G7 Master color management.
Is serialization handled in house?+
Yes. EasyCode applies unit-level codes, aggregation and DSCSA reporting on the same line that prints and finishes the packaging, and every code is camera-verified by 100% in-line inspection.
Can Platinum Press hold our inventory?+
Climate-controlled, cGMP-compliant warehousing at the Fort Worth campus supports inventory programs with 2-day shipping to 90% of the US.
Do you support artwork and prepress?+
Value-Added Services covers custom design and artwork support, G7-calibrated proofing, rapid prototyping and specification assistance, so files arrive at press ready to run.
What are typical lead times?+
Standard programs run 3 to 4 weeks from approved artwork. Warehousing programs shorten reorders further by shipping from stock.
