Folding Cartons

Pharmaceutical Folding Cartons

Contact Sales

Platinum Press produces folding cartons built for regulated products: standard cartons, secondary blister cartons and specialty constructions engineered to protect the product, carry the regulatory story and run clean on automated lines. Every carton is produced in cGMP facilities and passes 100% in-line vision inspection before it ships.

The flagship is the patented Combination Carton: carton and blister combined into one secure unit, cutting component count, protecting dosing accuracy and removing an entire assembly step from packing lines.

Structure is only half the job. Cartons print on two Koenig and Bauer Rapida presses under G7 Master color, with embossing, cold foil and coatings applied in line, and tamper-evident and serialized constructions available for brand protection programs.

Pair cartons with labels, literature and inserts and warehousing and fulfillment for a single-source program, one partner from concept to delivery. Serving prescription brands, medical device, OTC and consumer health.
