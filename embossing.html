Finishing · Embossing

Dimension You Can Feel

Contact Sales

Embossing gives packaging a third dimension: raised and recessed elements that make a carton unmistakable in hand and premium on shelf.

Precision is what separates craft from craft-ish: emboss registered to print and foil, depth held consistent across the run, and structures engineered so dimension never compromises the board.

Combined with cold foil, embossing produces the flagship effect: dimensional metallic that photographs flat and stuns in person.

See the rest of the toolkit at Finishing, and where it lands: personal care, OTC and premium carton programs.
