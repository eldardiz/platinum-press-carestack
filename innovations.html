Innovations the Public Giants Do Not Have

Five packaging technologies engineered in-house at Platinum Press to protect product, brand and patient, from the patented Combination Carton to serialized anti-counterfeiting.

Patented · Folding Cartons

Combination Carton: Two Components, One Secure Unit

Integrates a secondary blister directly into the folding carton.
Fewer components, fewer line steps, faster packing speeds.
Tamper evidence and dosing accuracy built into the structure.
Produced in cGMP facilities with 100% in-line vision inspection.

Combines carton and blister into one patented unit, cutting component count on every run.

Protects dosing accuracy with a structure designed around the patient, not just the product.

Speeds packing lines by removing an entire assembly step from the workflow.

⬚
Serialization · DSCSA

EasyCode: Serialization Built for DSCSA

Unit-level coding printed and verified in a single pass.
Aggregation from unit to case to pallet, documented throughout.
Reporting that plugs into your trading partners and auditors.
Every code camera-verified by 100% in-line vision inspection.

Serializes every unit with variable data that scans the first time, every time.

Aggregates unit, case and pallet relationships so chain of custody survives the supply chain.

Reports in the formats your DSCSA trading partners actually accept.

⊛
Security · Anti-Counterfeiting

CodeShield: Protection Seen and Unseen

Overt features patients and pharmacists can verify at a glance.
Covert features only your supply chain knows to look for.
Anti-diversion tracking integrated with serialization data.
Applied in the same print pass, no second operation.

Deters counterfeiters with layered features that are expensive to copy and easy to verify.

Flags diverted product by tying every unit's identity to its intended channel.

▤
Literature · Automation

Flatsert: High-Density Literature, Flat Format

Carries full regulatory content in a flat, machine-friendly format.
Runs on automated lines without jams or misfeeds.
Multi-language support without ballooning the folded bulk.
Printed with FDA-ready accuracy and inspected 100%.

Packs more patient information into less space than folded inserts and outserts.

Feeds cleanly through high-speed packing equipment, cutting downtime on the line.

◈
Traceability · Authentication

Source Tags: Identity Embedded in Every Unit

Embedded tags carry authentication data inside the packaging itself.
Verification without special hardware at the point of dispense.
Pairs with EasyCode and CodeShield for layered protection.
Invisible to counterfeiters, obvious to your supply chain.

Embeds traceability into the unit so identity travels with the product, not the paperwork.

Verifies authenticity anywhere in the chain, from warehouse to pharmacy shelf.
