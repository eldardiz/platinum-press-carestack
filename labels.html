Labels

Pharmaceutical Label Printing

Contact Sales

Platinum Press produces pharmaceutical labels built for regulated products: pressure-sensitive, extended content and booklet formats engineered to carry more information, survive the supply chain and stay compliant. Every label runs in cGMP facilities and passes 100% in-line vision inspection before it ships, so what reaches your line is what your quality team approved.

Print options go as deep as your brand needs: 10-color capability, braille and tactile features, cold foil and specialty finishes, all held to G7 Master color run after run. For products with sustainability mandates, our VOC and PFAS-free digital printing covers short runs and versioning without compromising compliance.

Labels are where serialization lives. Platinum Press labels arrive serialization-ready, with EasyCode unit-level coding and CodeShield anti-counterfeiting available in the same pass, backed by aggregation and DSCSA reporting from our Serialization team.

For the ultimate single-source program, pair labels with folding cartons, literature and inserts and warehousing and fulfillment, one partner from concept to delivery. Serving prescription brands, medical device, OTC and consumer health.

Extended Content Labels

Booklet Labels
