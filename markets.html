FAQ

Does every market get the same quality system?−
Yes. Whether the product is a prescription drug or a cosmetic, it runs through the same cGMP facilities, the same G7-calibrated presses and the same 100% in-line vision inspection.
Can one program span several markets?+
Yes. Brands with portfolios across Rx, OTC and wellness consolidate them into one program with one spec owner and one audit trail.
Which market pages should I read first?+
Start with the market closest to your product. Pharma / Prescription covers serialization depth, Medical Device covers UDI labeling, and both link to the products and capabilities they rely on.
My category is not listed. Can Platinum Press still help?+
Usually, yes. If the product is regulated, labeled or dosed, the platform likely covers it. Contact Sales with your requirements and a packaging expert will confirm fit.
