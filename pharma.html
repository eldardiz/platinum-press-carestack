How does Platinum Press support DSCSA serialization? −
EasyCode handles unit-level coding, aggregation and reporting, and 100% in-line vision inspection verifies every code as it runs. Programs ship with the documentation your trading partners and auditors expect.
What does 100% in-line vision inspection actually mean? +
Every unit, not a sample, passes camera verification on the line. Defects are rejected automatically and the inspection record stays with the batch.
How fast can a new Rx program launch? +
New programs average 3-4 week lead times after artwork approval, with 2-day shipping coverage to 90% of the United States from our centrally located facilities.
Which certifications does Platinum Press hold? +
Our facilities operate under cGMP and hold ISO 9001:2015, G7 Master and SGS&Co certifications. Certificates are available on request through Contact Sales.
Can Platinum Press take over an existing program mid-cycle? +
Yes. Transfers follow a documented onboarding plan covering artwork, serialization scope and inventory, so continuity of supply is protected.
