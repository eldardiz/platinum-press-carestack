FAQ

Why Platinum Press for Regulated Packaging

View All Queries →
Can Platinum Press run every format in one program?−
Yes. Labels, cartons, literature, lidding, flexible packaging and security products all run under one quality system, so a consolidated program keeps a single spec owner, a single audit trail and a single point of accountability.
How does serialization work across product families?+
EasyCode applies unit-level coding, aggregation and DSCSA reporting on labels and cartons alike, and every serialized code is verified by 100% in-line vision inspection before shipment.
What are typical lead times?+
Standard programs run 3 to 4 weeks from approved artwork, with 2-day shipping to 90% of the US from our Fort Worth facilities.
Can you match an existing spec from another supplier?+
Yes. Our prepress and quality teams re-qualify incoming specs against your requirements, produce proofs for approval and document the transition so switching suppliers never risks a launch date.
Which certifications back the product lines?+
cGMP operations, ISO 9001:2015, G7 Master color qualification and SGS&Co certification, with certificates available to qualified buyers on request.
